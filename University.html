A thriving campus replete with state-of-the-art infrastructure and tree-lined avenues sets the stage for learning in classrooms and beyond. CGU Odisha is diverse in its offerings and prides itself on being an innovation hub where young minds come together to learn, inspire and innovate.

Infrastructure at CGU is constantly evolving with new and modern facilities and equipment being added all the time to facilitate the student journey. A sprawling premise housing some of the most equipped laboratories, libraries, academic buildings and auditorium is worthy of a visit. For fun and recreation.

The university’s commitment to academic brilliance is reflected in its remarkable achievements. Ranked 11th overall and 9th in research capability in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), CV Raman Global University continues to lead with a relentless focus on innovation and excellence.

CV Raman Global University’s vision goes beyond academic success. It aims to create leaders, innovators, and entrepreneurs who will shape the future. With a strong foundation and an unwavering commitment to excellence, the university continues to inspire its students to dream big and achieve even bigger.

As a proud student of this institution, I’ve experienced firsthand the transformative impact it has on its community. From the inspiring lectures to the collaborative projects, every moment at CV Raman Global University is a step toward achieving greatness. This is not just a university; it’s a journey of discovery, growth, and endless possibilities.
